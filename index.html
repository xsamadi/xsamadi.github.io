  • Mohammad Samadi
    R&D Software Specialist

Mohammad Samadi received ASc in Computer Software in 2005, BSc in Software Engineering in 2009, and MSc in Computer Engineering in 2013. He is a researcher at Polytechnic Institute of Porto and a PhD candidate at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya - BarcelonaTech.
Mohammad has developed many software systems and is the author of over 40 scientific publications. He was a lecturer of university for about 7 years and an advisor to over 30 graduate students. Furthermore, Mohammad has been an editor, TPC member, and reviewer in international journals and conference proceedings.
